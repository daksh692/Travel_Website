Our story

Travel started as a simple idea: build tools that feel fast, make sense, and look great. We iterated openly, improving layout, states, and feedback at every step.

  1. Consistent UI foundation

    Unified header/nav and base styles ensure every page feels familiar and responsive, with clear spacing and readable typography.

  2. Explore with interactive maps

    A dynamic India SVG map leads to state pages. Hover and tap cues, smooth transitions, and accessible labels make discovery intuitive.

  3. Transportation that flows

    Two tabs: Booking and Already Booked (self-added trips). A 2-column card grid avoids overlap; buttons and totals stay visible and legible on any screen.

  4. Deals that stay put

    Bundled places with transparent pricing and a random 5–15% discount. Each surfaced deal remains stable for 72 hours and biases 1–2 deals toward your most-visited state.

  5. Loyalty & Account clarity

    Loyalty points with a clean Redeem flow. Account pages have accessible validation, specific error messages, and a friendly password-change popover for instant feedback.
